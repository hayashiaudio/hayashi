Current Behavior: Continuous Sources (Oscillator/Noise)

Looking at the current code:

  • Oscillator and noise tracks are NOT auto-created when you connect them (they're in SKIP_AUTO_TRACK_KINDS)
  • But if you manually add a track and wire it to an oscillator, it appears as a "continuous" track with a Bounce button
  • The handleBounceTrack function renders 4 beats of oscillator/noise output into a WAV clip via OfflineAudioContext
  • They do NOT show as live running clips — just a track header with a bounce button. The live audio plays through the graph, not as a clip on the timeline
  • The user can click Print (for sample-based sources) or Bounce (for continuous sources) to freeze them into actual clips

So no — processor effects like oscillator are not rendered as ongoing clips. They exist as live audio graph nodes. The workstation shows their track lane, but the lane is empty until you bounce/print them.
